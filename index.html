Eldritch Horror Helper

Many fans of board games have heard about Eldritch Horror as one of the most interesting board adventures, but there is one difficulty: preparing for the game for inexperienced players takes from 40 minutes to an hour of time and one of the most difficult conditions is the compilation of a deck of myths.


This application is designed to compile a deck of myths.


You will be given 4 ancient ones to choose from, each of which has its own set of cards.


Each ancient has 5 difficulty levels, depending on these difficulty levels you will have a deck.
